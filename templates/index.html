Automatically track and analyze your microscope camera feed in real-time

  1. Click "Start Screen Share" and select the microscope mounted camera output window
  2. Once sharing begins, click and drag on the screen to select the specific region containing your microscope view
  3. A live preview of your selected region will appear in the right panel. Use "Reset Selection" to adjust if needed, or "Stop Sharing" when done
  4. Proceed with your normal BMA interpretation workflow! The selected region will be automatically monitored and analyzed by BMACopilot
